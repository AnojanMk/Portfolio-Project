The Best Movies According to Angela

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Spirited Away

This is my favourite anime. I love the beautiful images.

Spirited Away became the highest-grossing film in Japanese history at the time and is widely regarded as one of the greatest animated films ever made. It introduced global audiences to the artistry of Studio Ghibli and helped elevate anime as a respected cinematic form

Ex Machina

Really cool sci-fi movie.

Ex Machina is a sleek sci-fi thriller where a young programmer is invited to test a humanoid AI named Ava. As he interacts with her, questions of consciousness, control, and manipulation unfold. The film explores the eerie boundaries between human and machine.

Drive

Super beautiful film. Really artistic.

Drive follows a quiet Hollywood stuntman who moonlights as a getaway driver in Los Angeles. When he tries to help his neighbor with a dangerous job, things spiral into violence and betrayal. The film blends minimal dialogue, neon visuals, and haunting intensity.
